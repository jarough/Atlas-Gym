Our Facilities:

Personal Trainers

If you are a new gym member or even a long-time member, our experienced personal trainers can always help you take your workout to the next step. They can assist in creating safe and effective regimes designed to each individual as well as offering support and motivation throughout the program.

Benefits to having a personal trainer are that you get to learn correct workout form to prevent injury, gain more knowledge about which routines benefit you the most, and they can help set goals both short and long term.

Interested in taking on a personal trainer? Please enquire at reception.

Weights and Equipment

We have a wide variety of equipment to use across our gym, from cardio equipment to weights.
In the Main Gym, we house treadmills, cross trainers, exercise bikes and rowing machines for those looking for a cardio workout, alongside typical weights equipment such as dumbbells, lifting benches and squat racks for those looking to build muscle.

Our Facilities:

Weights

We have a wide variety of weights to choose from in the Main Gym. Ranging from plates and barbells to lifting benches, there is something for everyone in terms of specific fitness and muscular goals.

Swimming Pool

NEW! Installed Summer 2023

We are proud to show off our new indoor swimming pool, featuring 4 lanes and varying depths for all ages. It is open to all members of the gym for activities such as leisurely swimming, swimming lessons, water aerobics and team games.

Activity Studio

The activity studio is home for all types of group and personalised activities as well as some individual ones. These are things such as yoga and zumba classes, along with dance classes, but may also be used for small group workouts.

Cafe

Our gym cafe is the perfect place to wind down after a long workout. We cater to a wide range of preferences and all ages. From items such as sandwiches and snacks to coffees and teas, there is something for everyone.
